The Simpsons is one of my favorite shows! There are a lot of reasons to love it! Below are some of the main reasons that I feel the show has been successful.
  1. Characters
    • The characters are based on simple overgeneralized stereotypes, making them easy to reuse from episode to episode.
    • The characters often represent entire groups with their single character, making it possible to make large scale jokes about society.
  2. Plot
    • The Simpsons is full of halfway plot switches. Starting off with one plot each episode typically pivots to a loosly related second plot.
    • Episodes often poke fun at societal issues or relateable situations
  3. Predictions
    • The simpsons has predicted numererous world events long before they actually happened

In a way the Simpsons represents society. Sometimes the worst of it, and sometimes the best of it. It's a great show.
